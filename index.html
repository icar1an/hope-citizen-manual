1

atmospheric obscuration surges

Atmospheric Obscuration Surges (commonly: Surges) are periods of total darkness caused by dense particulate clouds descending upon populated zones. During a Surge, all visible light is absorbed. Outdoor movement becomes impossible.

threat classification

advisory

Reduced visibility. Outdoor activity discouraged.

warning

Significant obscuration. Return indoors immediately.

critical

Total darkness. Siren will sound. Lock all entry points.

Duration varies. Average: 6-8 hours. Maximum recorded: 72 hours (Year 845 A.Dk.)

2

the phased / lucid / lost

All citizens are classified under the Phase Identification System. During Surge conditions, the Watchlight beam reveals an individual's current phase state.

unphased

Solid beam reflection. Individual is present and accounted for. Standard citizen status.

no action required

lucid

Beam passes through. Individual is partially phased. Maintain distance. Report to sector authority.

observe and report

lost

Figure disperses under beam. Individual is no longer present. Do not approach. Seal the area.

do not engage
3

siren procedure

When the Night Siren sounds, execute the following procedure without delay. Compliance is mandatory. Deviation endangers all citizens within your sector.

step 01

recognize the siren

The Night Siren emits a rising-falling tone cycle. When heard, cease all activity.

step 02

proceed indoors

Move to the nearest designated shelter or your assigned dwelling. Close all openings.

step 03

lock and illuminate

Secure all entry points. Activate your Watchlight. Monitor windows and doorways.

step 04

await morning signal

Remain in position until the Morning Signal sounds. Do not exit before confirmation.

4

morning return

The Morning Signal confirms the end of Surge conditions. Citizens must follow the Verification Protocol before resuming normal activity.

observe

Check windows for visible light. Confirm natural daylight has returned to your sector.

verify

Listen for the Morning Signal — three ascending tones. This confirms H.O.P.E. authorization to proceed.

permit

Unlock entry points. Exit is now permitted. Report any anomalies to your sector coordinator.
